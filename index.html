About Me

I am Prayas Chandra Patel, a scientist with expertise in sustainable materials for energy and data technologies. My research spans advanced energy materials, thermoelectrics, high-entropy alloys, spintronics, quantum materials, and Li/Na-ion batteries. I am passionate about finding innovative solutions to the world's energy challenges through the lens of solid-state physics and materials science.

Research

My research focuses on the development of sustainable materials and technologies, particularly in the realm of solid-state batteries, thermoelectrics, and spintronics. I am also actively engaged in exploring high-entropy alloys and their potential for advancing quantum materials and energy storage solutions.

  • Solid-state Li/Na-ion batteries
  • High-entropy alloys for energy technologies
  • Spintronics and quantum materials
  • Sustainable energy solutions and thermoelectrics

Teaching

As an aspiring faculty member at IIT Delhi, I am committed to fostering interdisciplinary learning and research in physics and materials science. My teaching philosophy centers around experiential education, integrating research into the classroom, and promoting real-world applications. I also strive to mentor students and encourage diversity, equity, and inclusion in the academic environment.
